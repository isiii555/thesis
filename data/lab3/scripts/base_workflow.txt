@startuml
title Base Workflow: From Raw Logs to Insights (No-LLM Baseline)

start
:Collect raw system/app/infra logs;
:Preprocess & Normalize
- parse timestamps
- unify schema
- remove noise;

if (Correlation keys present?\n(e.g., requestId, sessionId)) then (Yes)
  :Group by correlation keys;
else (No)
  :Mine message templates;
  :Cluster similar events;
  :Apply temporal windowing;
endif

:Build traces (caseId, activity, timestamp, resource,...);
:Export event log (XES/CSV);

:Run process discovery
- Inductive Miner
- Heuristic Miner;

:Evaluate model
- fitness
- precision
- generalization;

:Compute performance KPIs
- throughput
- waiting times
- bottlenecks;

:Generate dashboards & report;
:Create improvement actions & backlog;

:Feedback: re-collect logs for next cycle;
stop
@enduml
